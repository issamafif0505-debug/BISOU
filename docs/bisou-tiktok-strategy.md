## 5. CONTENT PILLARS (The 80/20 Rule) 🎯

### Distribution Formula:

```
📊 BISOU Content Mix:

🎉 50% ENTERTAINMENT (Trends, Memes, Humor)
   ├─ Trend participation (dance, challenges, memes)
   ├─ Humor/relatable content ("BISOU girl logic")
   ├─ Duets/Stitches with trending creators
   ├─ Funny customer testimonials
   └─ Pop culture references (humor angle)

📚 30% EDUCATION (How-tos, Tips, Behind-Scenes)
   ├─ How to layer jewelry
   ├─ Jewelry care tips
   ├─ Styling guides
   ├─ Behind-the-scenes production
   └─ Outfit combination ideas

💰 20% SALES (Product Showcase, Testimonials, Promos)
   ├─ Product showcase/close-ups
   ├─ Customer testimonials
   ├─ Flash sales/promotions
   ├─ Unboxing videos
   └─ Limited edition drops
```

### Weekly Distribution Example:

```
MONDAY:
✓ 1 Entertainment (GRWM trend)

TUESDAY:
✓ 1 Sales (Unboxing)
✓ (Bonus: 1 Education if capacity)

WEDNESDAY:
✓ 1 Education (Tutorial)

THURSDAY:
✓ 1 Entertainment (Trend challenge)
+ Engagement push (replies to comments)

FRIDAY:
✓ 1 Sales (Flash sale)
+ Challenge launch

SATURDAY:
✓ Repost 1 UGC/customer video

SUNDAY:
✓ Plan next week + engage comments
```

### Why This Mix Works for BISOU:

- **50% Entertainment:** TikTok algorithm = entertainment first. People scroll for fun, not to buy. Entertainment = views + followers
- **30% Education:** Trust building. "BISOU knows jewelry" positioning. Lower sales friction (educated buyer = faster conversion)
- **20% Sales:** Direct sales content. Not overwhelming. Positioned as "value" not "hard sell"

**Key Rule:** Never post 2 sales videos in a row. Always buffer with entertainment/education.

## 8. ANALYTICS TRACKING 📊

### Core KPIs to Monitor (Weekly + Monthly)

```
🎯 TIER 1 METRICS (Most Important):

Views (Total Reach):
├─ Target: 1,000 views/video minimum
├─ Track: Weekly average (ex: 15,000 views across 3 videos = 5K avg)
├─ Goal Month 1: 3,000-5,000 avg views/video
├─ Goal Month 3: 10,000+ avg views/video
└─ Reality: Month 1 videos might be 500-2K, it's normal

Engagement Rate (Quality Metric):
├─ Formula: (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Views × 100
├─ Healthy Range: 3-8% engagement = good
├─ Below 1% = content not resonating
├─ Above 10% = viral goldmine (keep repeating)
├─ Track: Per video to identify what works
└─ Goal: Average 5%+ engagement rate by month 3

Shares (Most Important Engagement):
├─ Why: Shares > Comments > Likes (in algorithm weight)
├─ Target: Aim for 10+ shares per video minimum
├─ Indicates: Content is valuable (people sharing to friends)
└─ High share content = next viral video candidate

Profile Views:
├─ Growth Indicator: How many people checking your profile
├─ Target: 500+ profile views/week
├─ Conversion: 10-20% of profile viewers = followers
└─ If low: Means videos getting seen but not good hooks
```

```
🎯 TIER 2 METRICS (Secondary but Important):

Follower Growth:
├─ Track: Weekly growth (ex: +50 followers/week)
├─ Goal Month 1: +200-500 followers
├─ Goal Month 3: +2,000+ followers
├─ Reality Check: Growth accelerates over time
└─ Benchmark: Jewelry brands average 5-10% monthly growth

Watch Time (Average):
├─ Shows: How long people watching your video
├─ Target: 50%+ watch time (if 30sec video = 15sec watched)
├─ Insight: Long watch time = good retention (hook working)
├─ If low (<30%): Hook is weak, people scrolling fast
└─ Improve: Better hook or stronger first 3 seconds

Click-Through Rate (Link in Bio):
├─ Formula: (Clicks to link) / Views × 100
├─ Target: 2-5% CTR is good (100 views = 2-5 link clicks)
├─ What it tracks: How many people actually interested in buying
├─ Industry standard: 1-3% is normal for e-commerce
└─ Improve: Stronger CTA in video + optimized bio

Traffic to WhatsApp:
├─ Track: Via WhatsApp number (manually or link shortener with analytics)
├─ Goal: 10+ WhatsApp chats/week from TikTok
├─ Conversion: 20-30% of WhatsApp chats = actual sales
├─ Most Important for BISOU (WhatsApp = your sales channel)
└─ Target: 20+ WhatsApp chats/week by week 3
```

```
🎯 TIER 3 METRICS (Monitor but Less Critical):

Comments Count:
├─ Quality > Quantity
├─ Track: What people saying (insight into audience)
├─ 50+ comments = video resonated emotionally
└─ Read comments: They tell you what people want

Follower Source:
├─ TikTok shows: Where followers coming from (which videos)
├─ Insight: Identify your best performing content
└─ Action: Double down on those video types

├─ List: All videos by views (best to worst)
```
